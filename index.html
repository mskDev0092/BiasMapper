About BiasMapper

What is BiasMapper?

BiasMapper is an innovative framework designed to analyze and classify content across multiple dimensions of bias. Unlike traditional left-right spectrums, our model incorporates vertical (top-down vs bottom-up) perspectives for more comprehensive analysis.

Why It Matters

In today's complex information landscape, understanding bias is crucial for media literacy, critical thinking, and balanced decision-making. BiasMapper provides a nuanced approach that goes beyond simple partisan classifications.

Our Mission

To promote fairness, inclusivity, and balanced analysis by making bias visible and understandable.

The Bias Framework

L (Left)
R (Right)
T (Traditional)
B (Bottom-up)
C (Centrist)

Each primary bias has intensity levels:

  • + Moderate intensity (e.g., L+, R+)
  • ++ Strong intensity (e.g., L++, R++)
  • No symbol indicates neutral position within that category
