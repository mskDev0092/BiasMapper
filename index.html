The BiasMapper Framework

BiasMapper analyzes content along two primary axes: Ideological (Left to Right) and Societal (Topical to Baseline), with intensity levels ranging from neutral (C) to extreme (++).

Ideological Axis (Left/Right)

  • L++ Strong Left: Far-left political perspective with radical viewpoints
  • L+ Moderate Left: Center-left political perspective with progressive leanings
  • C Centrist: Balanced perspective with minimal ideological bias
  • R+ Moderate Right: Center-right political perspective with conservative leanings
  • R++ Strong Right: Far-right political perspective with radical viewpoints

Societal Axis (Topical/Baseline)

  • T++ Strong Topical: Focuses heavily on specific topics while ignoring others
  • T+ Moderate Topical: Emphasizes certain topics more than others
  • C Balanced: Even coverage of relevant topics
  • B+ Moderate Baseline: Subtle assumptions about foundational perspectives
  • B++ Strong Baseline: Assumes certain foundational perspectives as absolute truth

Frequently Asked Questions

BiasMapper achieves approximately 85-90% accuracy in identifying primary biases based on our validation studies. The confidence score provided with each analysis indicates our model's certainty about the results. For best accuracy, we recommend analyzing texts of at least 200 words.
Currently, BiasMapper works best with English text. We're actively developing models for Spanish, French, and German, with plans to expand to more languages in the future. Some bias detection features may work with other languages, but accuracy will be lower.
Our reframing algorithm identifies biased phrases and replaces them with alternative expressions that convey similar information but from different perspectives. The process maintains factual accuracy while adjusting tone, emphasis, and framing. For best results, review reframed content to ensure it meets your needs.
We prioritize user privacy. Text analyzed in the free tool is processed in memory and not permanently stored. For API users and enterprise customers, we offer data retention options to suit different privacy requirements. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
We offer several integration options: a web interface (this tool), a REST API for developers, and browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox. Enterprise customers can access custom integrations with CMS platforms like WordPress and publishing tools. Contact us for more information about API access and enterprise solutions.
